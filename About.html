Our Story

Launched in 2023, Exclusive is Ogbomosho premier online shopping marketplace with an active presence all over Nigeria. Supported by a wide range of tailored marketing, data, and service solutions, Exclusive boasts 10,500 sellers and 300 brands, catering to over 3 million customers across the region.

Exclusive's product catalog has expanded rapidly to encompass over 1 million offerings, showcasing an impressive rate of growth. With a diverse assortment spanning various categories, Exclusive provides an array of options for consumers, ensuring a satisfying and comprehensive shopping experience.
